How to Use?

    CVGenius is an AI-powered CV creation platform to advance your career. Here are the steps on how to use the site:
  • Account Creation:To start using CVGenius, you must first create an account. To register, click the "Create Account" button on the main page and fill in the required information.
  • Profile Information: Add your profile information after creating an account. Identify yourself in areas such as name, contact information, skills, education and experience. This information will be used in the creation of your CV.
  • CV Creation: CVGenius offers you user-friendly templates. To create a CV, click "Create CV" and select the template you want. Next, enter your information in the relevant fields. The system will automatically create your CV with artificial intelligence technology.
  • Personalization: You can use different editing options to personalize your created CV. You can adjust the font, color, layout and section order according to your wishes.
  • Review Recommendations: CVGenius provides real-time recommendations to optimize your CV. You can make your CV more impressive by taking these suggestions into account. Don't forget to use the checking tools provided by the system to check for language and spelling errors.
  • Download and Share: After completing your CV, you can download it in PDF format with the "Download" option. You can also share your CV created through CVGenius on social media platforms or add it directly to job applications.
